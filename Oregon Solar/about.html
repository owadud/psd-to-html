about our team

Since 2001, Oregon Solarworks has offered experienced, professional grade solar system design for Oregon residential, commercial, and off-grid customers. Our team is there for you from initial consultation through installation and maintenance - every step of the way we will take care of you.

Kirpal Khalsa
General Manager

Kirpal Khalsa has been studying, designing and installing solar systems since 2001 and has been a NABCEP Certified PV Installation Professional since 2008. Prior to his work at Oregon Solarworks he was the operations manager at Renewable Energy Systems for 7.5 years working alongside Kerry Whitehead.

Kirpal lives on his solar powered homestead with his family in Rogue River Oregon.

Tyrone Houck
Apprentice

Tyrone Houck is rapidly moving thru the Oregon Solar Apprenticeship program. He is getting lots of hands on experience installing and designing PV systems. He comes from an electrical background and is a valuable member of our team. He lives with his family outside of Cave Junction where he is building his off the grid solar powered home.
